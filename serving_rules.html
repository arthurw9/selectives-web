Serving Rules

Use this page to grant and limit student access to certain pages.
Use "materials" to serve the pre-registration materials. Use "schedule" to serve the schedule page.
Page types "preferences" and "verification" are not currently in use.
Example: 
- name: materials
  allow:
    - current_grade: 6
    - current_grade: 7
    - email: sam.gamgee@gmail.com
- name: schedule
  allow:
    - current_grade: 8
    - current_homeroom: 19
Required field:
  • name
Optional field:
  • allow
    • current_grade specifies one grade level such as 8, 7, or 6
    • current_homeroom
    • email
